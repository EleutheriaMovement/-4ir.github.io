Our Vision

We all can no longer deny that the world is changing,and gradually everything is being digitized and other careers are becoming obsolete, countries such as USA, China, Japan and even India teach their children technology at the early age, as a result they produce leaders in the technology related industries. where our world is facing many challenges such as pandemics and suffers safety issues it is clear that the time is near where our children will have to adjust into online learning. and when that time comes, we should be ready, our vision is to introduce children to technology as early as possible, so that they can be equipped with a vast knowledge to face the wave of the forth industrial revolution

Our Mission

While it is essential to equip our children with tech sklls, our aim also is to spark innovation in them, this is a very important part of our academy, the society is facing many challenges and problem solvers are in short supply, with the help of parents, we hope to achieve this big dream of ours. We are on a mission of teaching children fundamentals of technology and ICT systems, this involves computer programming, where they will be taught to design mobile apps and online applications. We have this interesting idea of creating an android app that will teach children in their home languages in townships and rural areas, but we thought it would be more interesting if they themselves created it, so through out the learning programme, these kids will be collaborating in building this app. The name of the app is FundaApp©, our current mandate is to create a grade 1 mobile app that will teach kids "Isizulu soqobo" and then move on to other languages aswell and thereafter continue with other grades
